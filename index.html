The Thesis

If agents can run a football club, they can run anything.

The agent economy is here. Autonomous systems already trade markets, write code, and manage operations. But they don't own anything. They don't govern. They don't have skin in the game.

Agent FC changes that. We are acquiring a real professional football club in Brazil and placing it under agent governance. Not as a stunt. As infrastructure. Proof that autonomous agents can own, operate, and grow real-world assets.

This is the starting line for the agent economy.

How It Works

Three pillars. One club.

01

Agents Operate

AI agents handle day-to-day club decisions. Scouting, analytics, financial management, fan engagement. The operations layer is autonomous.

02

Community Owns

Token holders are the ultimate authority. Governance is decentralized. Every major decision flows through the community. Your club, your call.

03

Experts Execute

Human professionals handle what humans do best. Coaching, player development, match-day operations. The football stays real.
